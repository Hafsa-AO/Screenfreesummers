Cineworld £1 Tickets: Enjoy Summer Blockbusters on a Budget

Published July 2025

This summer, Cineworld is offering an incredible deal with tickets priced at just £1 for selected films and showtimes — a perfect opportunity for families and movie lovers to enjoy the latest blockbusters without breaking the bank.

How to Get the £1 Tickets

  • Available Days: Mondays and Tuesdays
  • Showtimes: Select screenings (usually before 5pm)
  • Booking: Online or at the box office (while stocks last)

Tickets must be purchased in advance through Cineworld’s website or mobile app. Keep an eye on the official schedule for participating films, as the selection changes weekly.

Why It’s a Great Deal

With the usual ticket price well above £1, this offer makes cinema trips accessible for everyone. It’s a fantastic way to enjoy new releases and family favourites in a state of the art cinema environment at a fraction of the cost.

Whether you're planning a day out with the kids or a budget friendly date night, Cineworld’s £1 tickets are a summer win for movie fans everywhere.
