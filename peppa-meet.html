Peppa Pig Meet & Greet at Battersea Power Station

Published July 2025

This week, everyone’s favourite piggy is making free meet and greet appearances at the Peppa Pig Store inside London’s Battersea Power Station. Perfect for little fans, Peppa will be there every Saturday and Sunday from 12 pm to 6 pm, with breaks in between. Photography with the star and big smiles included!

The store is full of themed experiences including pretend muddy puddles, a plush London bus for rides, soft play seating, and live storytime sessions featuring the exclusive Peppa Goes to London book. Episodes of Peppa Pig also play on a giant screen all day long.

For families visiting midweek, the Lift 109 chimney experience includes Peppa storytime on select days. Sessions run July 28–30 and August 4–6 at 10:30 am and 11:15 am. Booking ahead is recommended at lift109.co.uk.

Bring your camera, your wellies, and your best snort it’s an oinktastic summer day out for Peppa lovers!

Want to join Peppa and her friends? Find out more details and book your free visit on the Battersea Power Station events page.
